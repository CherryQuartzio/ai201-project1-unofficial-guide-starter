Title: Any opinions on ACC housing?
I'm choosing between VDCN deluxe, Camino del Sol Townhome, and Plaza Verde. What are some pros and cons of these housings? Also, which is the cleanest and quietest? I also heard that Camino doesn't have great wifi, but is it still that way?
--- COMMENTS ---
[comment]
Aside from floorpans, I feel like the different communities are really quite similar. They're all really clean, they all have bad wifi, and they're all pretty quiet. They're also all located on the same block so there's not a major difference between like whose closer to campus, esp since we have a bus system that goes to all of them.
      Plaza Verde will probably be the cleanest and the quietest since it's the newest place, but they also suffer a shortage of parking, if that's important to you. VDCN probably has the best parking, though.

I live in Camino and it's pretty nice. Feels like I'm just living in an apartment, if that makes sense. No real Uni feel. I should note: I've only lived here during covid and both of my roomates canceled their lease so I'm living alone. Not sure how thin the walls are n stuff like that.
      I like having my own bathroom and the kitchen is a great place to work on school stuff.
      If I could do it all again? I'd go for something cheaper I think. I can't discount how cool it is to have a bathroom attached to my room tho.
      Happy hunting! Ask me anything if you think of something!
