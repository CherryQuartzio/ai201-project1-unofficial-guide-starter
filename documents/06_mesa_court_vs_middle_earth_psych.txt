Title: Mesa Court vs Middle Earth
I’m Psychology BA major and a new student!
      I’m thinking about Middle Earth since it seems closest and I’m afraid the walk from Mesa Court to Social Sciences is not worth it despite the facilities being newer. Is the living quality in Mesa Court appealing enough that it deserves the extra long trip?
      Overall, which residential hall do you guys think is better? Thanks (:

room wise: middle earth classics and mesa classics are both ok. nothing special nothing terrible. you have to deal with communal bathrooms and mid rooms but if you really don’t want to live in a quad it’s not a bad option. i’d recommend the towers tho. mesa towers are really nice especially the newest one oso. middle earth towers are honestly even nicer in my opinion, i think everything is just a little bit more spacious and the layout is more convenient.
      social wise: mesa court is SO much more social than middle earth. being away from the classrooms makes it like that so i’d honestly recommend mesa.
      dining wise: anteatery (mesa) and brandywine (middle earth) have similar food but i def like the layout of anteatery better.
      walking wise: i’m also a psych major and honestly the walk is not too bad. first of all your classes will be spread all over, i don’t think i’ve had a single class in social ecology LOL. middle earth is closer to most classes especially ones in social sciences but mesa is honestly not that terrible of a walk. i’ve had some classes in biosci area and humanities and that’s lowk closer to mesa than middle earth. regardless you’ll have to walk no matter what and i don’t think the walk is that bad from mesa if you’re looking for a more social environment!
